Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need fasting for blood tests?

It depends on the test. Most routine tests require 10-12 hours fasting. Some tests don't require fasting. We'll inform you during booking.

How long does home collection take?

Usually 5-10 minutes. Our technologist will come with sterile equipment, collect the sample, and leave. Total time is minimal.

When will I get my reports?

Reports are usually ready within 24-48 hours. You'll receive a WhatsApp notification and digital copy first, followed by physical reports.

Is the collection process painful?

No, we use expert technique for painless collection. Our experience ensures minimal discomfort during blood withdrawal.

What areas do you cover for home visits?

We cover Hadapsar, Mundhwa, Kharadi, and Magarpatta. For other areas, please call and we'll check availability.

Can I get the reports interpreted?

Yes, basic interpretation is provided with reports. For detailed medical advice, consult with your doctor.
